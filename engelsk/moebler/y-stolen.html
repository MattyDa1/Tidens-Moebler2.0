  The Y-Chair

Y-stolen

Hans J. Wegners Y-Stolen

The Y-Stolen was designed by Hans J. Wegner in 1949 and has since then been in production at Carl Hansen & son, to this day. The Y-Chair's timeless design has built the foundation for generations of love for it coveted chair. The chair comes in beech and oak, and can be chosen in countless colors according to one's mood. CH24 the chair, better known as the Y-Stolen due to its light sculptural design, is a classic in Danish homes, the chair is perfect as a dining table chair, but is also extremely comfortable for other uses.

Specifications

Height: 76 cm
Depth: 51 cm
Width: 55 cm
