About Tidens Møbler

Welcome to Tidens Møbler. We are a store with a proud history since our foundation in 2001 by Morten Broløs. With a location in the heart of Aarhus, we welcome you to our unique space, where you can explore our extensive range of designer furniture, lighting and home accessories.


When you enter our store, you will experience an inspiring environment filled with beautifully designed furniture that reflects our passion for quality and aesthetics. We want to create an experience that goes beyond just that buy furniture, and therefore we give you the opportunity to read about many of our design furniture here on our Homepage. That way, you can start exploring and considering your options before visiting us.

Our values

We strive to give our customers a personal, present and inspiring experience. We wish to build long-term relationships based on honesty, trust and quality. With us you will experience a service that goes beyond the expected and we look forward to helping you create your dream space with our selection of designer furniture, lighting and home accessories.

Our Missions

Our mission at Tidens Møbler is to enrich our customers' lives by offering a unique collection of design furniture and create memorable experiences that last for many years.


Our mission also includes creating a personal and present service for each individual customer. We want to listen to your wishes and needs and offer professional advice and guidance through the entire purchase process. We strive to create a trusting relationship and ensure that our customers feel feel valued and taken care of throughout their journey with us.

Vores visioner

We want to inspire our customers to create unique and beautiful spaces with the help of our wide selection of designer furniture and home goods. We want to be a source of inspiration in furniture design and interior design and to allow our customers to express their personal style and aesthetics through our products.


Our vision also includes creating an inspiring and pleasant environment in our store where customers can experience the furniture in its proper element and be inspired by various design possibilities. We strive to create an atmosphere of welcome where customers feel valued, seen and heard.
