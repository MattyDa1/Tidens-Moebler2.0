How do I best maintain my furniture?

1. Cleaning: Dust and clean your furniture regularly. Use a soft cloth or a vacuum cleaner with a soft brush to remove dust, fluff and dirt. Avoid that use aggressive cleaning agents as they can damage the furniture surface.

2. Stains and spills: If you spill liquid or get a stain on your furniture, you must act quickly. Use a clean cloth or paper towel to dry up before it penetrates the wood or upholstery. If necessary, you can use a mild detergent intended for the specific material.

4. Protection from sunlight: Avoid placing your furniture in a place where it will be exposed for prolonged periods of direct sunlight. How can I choose the right style of furniture for my home?

When choosing furniture style for your home, it is important to consider your personal taste, the overall design of the room and the functionality of the furniture. You can consider creating a coherent style by choosing furniture that matches the architectural style of the room elements and color scheme. Explore different styles like modern, classic, rustic, minimalist, etc. and look what that best suits your aesthetics and needs.

How do I measure correctly to ensure the furniture fits in my space?

To ensure that the furniture fits well in your room, you must measure both the width and the height and the depth of the accessible area. Use a measuring tape and make sure to measure from wall to wall wall. Remember to also take into account any doors, windows or other obstacles there can affect the placement of the furniture. It is also a good idea to make a sketch of the room and the measurements so you can visualize how the furniture will fit.

How do I care for my furniture to extend its life?

Caring for furniture depends on the material. In general, it is recommended to vacuum or dry the furniture regularly to remove dust and dirt. Use suitable cleaning agents and methods to the specific material, e.g. wood polishing for wooden furniture or leather cleaner for leather furniture. Avoid direct sunlight, excess heat and humidity, as this can affect the appearance of the furniture and durability. Always follow the manufacturer's instructions for care and cleaning of your specific furniture.

Which materials are best suited for outdoor furniture?

When it comes to outdoor furniture, materials such as aluminum, teak, synthetic fibers (e.g. rattan or polyrattan) and stainless steel are popular choices. These materials are known for their resilience against weather conditions such as sun, rain and humidity. It is also recommended to choose furniture with weather resistance cushions and covers that are easy to clean or can be removed and washed.
