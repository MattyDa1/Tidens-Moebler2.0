  The Gubi-Lite

Gubi-Lite Pendel

Louis Weisdorf Gubi-Lite Pendel

The lamp was designed by Louis Weisdorf and is called Multi-Lite, due to the many possibilities to adapt and personalize the lamp and give it a personal look, the idea of ​​the lamp is to be able to be creative with the modules, which are easy to move around. Multi-Line was designed in 1972, and created for the purpose of expressing oneself through one's own style, and make a product that fits into different homes.

Specifications

Height: 36 cm
Diameter: 36 cm
Cord length: 4 m
Light source: Max 57W E27
