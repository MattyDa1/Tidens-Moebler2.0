Flowerpot Table Lamp VP9

Verner Panton Flowerpot Table Lamp VP9

The VP9 table lamp, better known as the flowerpot, was designed by Verner Panton in 1968 and has been a classic ever since. the lamp is compact and can bring light into small corners, the clean and minimalist look makes the lamp perfect for most homes. It now comes as a USB version, which gives more flexibility in the home.
The lamp comes in a lot of different colours, and gives you the freedom to strike the mood you are looking for in the home.

Specifications

Materials: Polycarbonate
Height: 29,5 cm
Diameter: 160 cm
Light source: 3W LED - 325 Lumen - 2700k
